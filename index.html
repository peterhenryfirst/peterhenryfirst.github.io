  • Polyghtgons
    December 2013 – October 2014
    Polyghtgons is my end-of-master's project, developed using Ogre3D, PhysX, FMOD and Lua. I also developed a editor to create levels to the project with HTML5 (Canvas), Javascript and Nodejs.
    It's a PC puzzle game in which you take control of the Polyghtgons: light bulb-shaped characters whose sacred totem has been stolen by the shadows. These characters have two key elements: their head (a cube, a sphere, a pyramid) and their color (red, green, blue, and mixtures of them).
    Interact with the hexagonal-grid world and its entities by lighting Polyghtgons up to create new paths, clone light patterns, replay sound patterns and, finally, get to the exit of each level.
